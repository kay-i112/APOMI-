Our Story

A School Born
From Legacy

The Abbie Peal Okia Memorial Institute was founded to honor a remarkable life and to ensure that future generations of Black Liberian students receive world-class education rooted in faith, identity, and purpose.

📖

Academic Excellence

Rigorous curriculum aligned with Liberian Ministry of Education standards, preparing every student for WAEC, BECE, and university entrance.

✝️

Faith & Character

Grounded in Christian values, we build integrity, discipline, and moral character so graduates serve God and country with distinction.

🌍

Liberian Identity & Heritage

Our programs celebrate Liberian history, African heritage, and cultural pride — every student knows their roots and stands tall in who they are.

Campus Facilities

Our World-Class
Campus

Every space at APOIM is purpose-built to inspire curiosity, discipline, and excellence in every Liberian student.

🔬 Science & Computer Laboratories

Fully equipped biology, chemistry, physics, and computer science labs with 40 workstations and internet access. Students conduct hands-on experiments and research projects — building skills for STEM careers in Liberia and beyond.

3 Labs · 40 Computer Stations · Modern Equipment

🏫 Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-lit classrooms with chalkboards and student desks. Small class sizes ensure every learner receives individual attention from Kindergarten to Grade 12.

20 Classrooms · Max 35 Students

🍽️ Dining Hall & Lunch Area

A clean cafeteria serving fresh Liberian meals daily — rice, soup, greens, and protein. Safe, supervised indoor and outdoor shaded seating for all students.

Seats 250 · Hot Meals Daily · Mon–Fri

⚽ Sports & Athletics Ground

Open sports fields for football, athletics, and PE. Our sports program builds teamwork, discipline, and physical wellness alongside academic achievement.

Football · Track · PE · Basketball

📚 School Library

Over 1,000 textbooks, reference materials, and reading books in a quiet, resourceful environment. Students use it for research, study groups, and reading clubs.

1,000+ Volumes · Quiet Study Zone

🌳 Open Campus & Grounds

A secure, landscaped 5-acre campus with shaded gathering areas, assembly grounds, and green spaces for cultural events — a safe environment every family trusts.

Gated · Secure · 5 Acres
Curriculum

Academic Programs

Aligned with the Liberian Ministry of Education — Kindergarten through Grade 12, with a focus on excellence, faith, and purpose at every stage.

🔢

Mathematics

Foundational numeracy through advanced algebra, geometry, and statistics for senior students.

🧪

Science & Biology

Hands-on lab work in biology, chemistry, and physics from junior levels upward.

✍️

English Language

Reading, writing, comprehension, and literature — building confident, articulate communicators.

🌍

Liberian & African Studies

History, civics, geography, and social studies rooted in Liberian and African heritage.

💻

ICT & Computer Studies

Digital literacy, computer applications, and basic coding for the modern economy.

✝️

Christian Religious Studies

Bible studies, moral education, and character development grounded in Christian faith.

⚽

Physical Education

Structured sports, fitness, and health education for holistic student development.

🎓

WAEC / BECE Prep

Dedicated senior preparation for West African examinations and university entrance guidance.
